Captured

Screen Capture Sharing for Mac

Step 1: ⌘-⇧-5

Take a screen shot

Step 2: ⌘-V

Paste the link

Step 3?

There is no step 3.

Mac App Store | How To | Twitter | Email

No steps added. Take a screen shot and Captured will upload the image automatically, and copy the link to your clipboard.

The goal is to take a common task and stream line it to the absolute minimum possible. Allowing you to work faster, and spend more time thinking about your projects and not how you are going to share a picture. Captured's history keeps past links handy, so you don't have to stop working and go back.

Captured uploads screen shots to Imgur by default, but can also be configured to work with Dropbox, Amazon's S3 or your own SFTP Server.
